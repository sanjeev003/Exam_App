Why This Platform Exists

It all started here at NIT Uttarakhand, during our lab exams. We saw piles of answer sheets, stacks of paper wasted, and teachers spending long hours correcting them. The process felt heavy — on the environment, on time, and on effort.

That’s when the idea struck: why not bring our exams online? A platform that saves paper, reduces correction time, and makes the whole process smoother for both students and teachers. This is not just code — it’s a step toward sustainability and efficiency.

Dear students, this is the very first draft of my idea. Your experience matters — please share your thoughts, stories, and suggestions. Together, we can make this platform better, smarter, and truly ours.
